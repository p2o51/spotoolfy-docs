# Welcome to Spotoolfy

> Version: 1.3.1 | Last Updated: December 2024

Welcome to the Spotoolfy Documentation Center! Spotoolfy is a Spotify companion client built with Flutter, bringing you lyrics display, AI translation, note-taking, music insights, and a time machine feature. All designed with Material Design 3 style.

## Core Features

- **Real-time Lyrics** - Multi-source lyrics (NetEase, QQ Music, LRCLIB) with line-by-line scrolling and manual search
- **AI Translation** - Three translation styles (Faithful, Poetic, Machine) powered by Gemini
- **Note System** - Add notes and ratings to every song, record your music stories
- **Time Machine** - Rediscover songs you saved on this day in previous years
- **Music Insights** - AI analyzes your listening habits to generate personalized music personality
- **Poster Generation** - Create beautiful shareable posters for lyrics, notes, and albums
- **Dynamic Theming** - Automatically generate color schemes from album art
- **Multi-language** - English, Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, Japanese

## Requirements

- **Spotify Premium** - Required for full playback control functionality (device switching, etc.)
- **Free Users** - Can view lyrics, log in, and take notes, but cannot control playback
- **Google AI API Key** (Optional) - For lyrics translation and AI insights, free to obtain
